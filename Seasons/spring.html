Spring Time is the Best Time!

This is a picture of mine from Park Lake, MT. This lake is located in the mountains behind Clancy, MT. This place was mentioned as a great place to go camping and fishing. This year I rented kyaks and got to go around the entire lake fishing. The spring time was great for catching Artic Greylings.

Spring time is a weird time for MT. We can experience all four seasons in the span of 24 hours during this time of the year.
