Web Hosts Reviews

Hostgator

Host Gator comes with five free features.They are; Unmetered bandwidth, Domain registration, SSL Certificate, One-click installs and unmetered storage.

You also get 24/7/365 support, 99.9% uptime (meaning Hostgator makes sure your site stays up), the option to create an online stor to sell your products and codemaster panel to help you.

Hostgator has three levels of membership. The first one is $2.64 a month (this is an introductory offer). Next is a baby offer at $3.38 a month. The last membership offer is their business offer that costs $5.08 a month.

Hostinger

Hostinger is a hosting site that has three plans. They are Cloud Starup ($9.99), Cloud Professional ($18.99), Cloud Enterprise ($69.99). All these plans are paid in monthly installments.

Hostingers features are very similar across all plans. They all allow for 300 websites, range from 3, 6, and 12 GB of memory.

inmotion hosting

inmotion hosting is very similar to Hostgator. They both have endless bandwidth, domain registration, SSL Certificat and one-click installs.

inmotion hosting offers four plans. The plans are priced at $2.49, $4.99, $4.99 and $12.99. These plans are offered for three years. Three of the four plans will only allow for three year subscription.

Top Pick

My top pick is the hosting site Hostgator. I picked this site because it is the cheapest and allows for smaller time commitments.

This is a great starter web hosting site that looks very basic. This site is perfect for those that are just starting out developing web sites.
